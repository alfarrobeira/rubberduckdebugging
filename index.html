Rubber Duck Debugging

After you have tried it, you will say: "How could I ever live (or at least code) without it???".

Imagine yourself in the following situation: After hours of programming, you found the perfect solution for a super-complex problem. At least you thought you found it. You implemented everything very neat and nicely, but for whatever reason it would not work as expected. And you cannot figure out why. "WHY???"

Here comes your friend. The rubber duck. Sitting right in front of you and listening to everything you tell it so patiently. So go ahead, describe the problem, describe every single step you made to solve it.

Have you experienced the magic happening? Just by listening to you, your little yellow (or pink or black or whatsoever) friend solved your problem! You suddenly know why it wouldn't work before and what tiny mistake you made.

Now go ahead. The finish line is there. Happy coding!
